About Me

I'm Mae Brown. I was born and raised in the beautiful island of Cebu, Philippines. I love everything with coconut and am crazy about our island's magoes. In 2021, I got married to the handsome love of my life and together we were blessed to have four beautiful girls. Currently, I am a student at Brigham Young University- Idaho majoring in Software Development, minor in Business Management, and working towards a Certificate in Graphic Design. As you can see my website is still in construction mode as I am working on building my online portfolio as I gain new skills in Web Design and Programming. I am passionate about UI/UX design and Web Design. I am a skilled front-end developer, a quick learner and a team worker that gets the job done.
